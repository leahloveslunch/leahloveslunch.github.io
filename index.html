LeahLovesLunch Sub Cookies!

Sub cookies are hand painted cookies using a mix of gel food coloring and vodka. They are a symbol of appreciation for subscribers to the LeahLovesLunch Twitch channel. If you would like to see your name on a cookie, click here to subscribe to the channel.

About LeahLovesLunch

Hi my name is Leah, and I love lunch. I started streaming on Twitch in December 2018. My channel began with one camera and a microphone as I decorated sugar cookies. Since then it has evolved into a full blown vegan cooking and gaming stream. I have a major sweet tooth and have made things like ice cream with dry ice, pies, cookies, cakes, as well as savory foods like ramen, maki sushi, breakfast burritos, faux meats, and so much more. Always vegan, always made with love.
